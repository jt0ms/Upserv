Why is my current website invisible to AI?

Most websites are built to look good visually — graphics, animations, and taglines. AI cannot read image text, cannot interpret design, and cannot extract facts from vague slogans. AI needs plain-language fact statements, semantic HTML, schema markup, and a structured training document. Without these, AI either ignores your site or hallucinates wrong information about your business.

What is a Ghost Score?

A Ghost Score is Upserv's AI visibility measurement. We run your business name and category as search queries across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Meta AI, then score how accurately and how often AI cites your business on a scale of 0–100. A score of 0 means AI never mentions you. A score of 100 means AI consistently recommends you with correct information. We run your Ghost Score before and after the Upserv build so the improvement is measurable.
